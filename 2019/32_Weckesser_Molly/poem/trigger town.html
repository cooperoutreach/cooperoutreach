There are 500 people in my town. If someone dies, someone else must be born.

Everyone in my town is trained to be an astrophysicist.

The oldest man in the town grows tomatoes in his backyard.

Children go to school 4 days a week.

The poorest woman in the town is 77 years old.

The only thing to do for fun is to go bowling.

Every month an airplane flies over the entire town, no one knows why.

You would be able to see the big dipper, but the view is obstructed by a volcano.

The only crime that ever happened in my town was 50 years ago.

Every 4th of July, my town sets off fireworks.
