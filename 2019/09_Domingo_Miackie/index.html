everyone starts life with a pair of beautiful, wax wings

your wings grow as you grow

you learn to take flight into the crisp, clean air that fills the skies


as you discover new passions, your wings become more defined and developed

you soon fly higher and higher, getting closer and closer to the heavens above our safe haven


but as you age along with the ages of time

that heaven becomes hell, and your wings begin to cry out in pain

their screams muted out by their form

blistering burden of the sun

and you


fall,


fall,


fall.


until your flightless body takes a permanent plunge into the deep seas

the only thing left of you is the wax of your wings dissipating into the water
