The Winter 2019 Outreach Program Collage Explorations | Basic HTML With Guest Partner Ava’s Smile

For Outreach’s Winter 2019 class, we explored both the possibilities and limitations of translating collage and poetry into engaging web experiences. Our collages and the poetry we generated with Dave Johnson informed and molded our final websites. Using language and 2D visuals, we experimented what was lost, gained, and amplified in the digital context.

For most, this was the first time expressing themselves in code. During the class we defined what a digital space is, how to structure it by creating sitemaps and how to distort it through often mischievous user flows. Students mastered the foundations of web development through HTML and CSS, which they used to code the portfolio sites you see on your left.

At the end of the five weeks, students created 4 works collage and poetry, a website which was on view in Cooper Union’s public student exhibition.
